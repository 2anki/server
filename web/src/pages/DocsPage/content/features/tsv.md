---
title: TSV
description: TSV support on 2anki.net
---

Tab-separated values are not currently accepted by the upload form. If you have a `.tsv` file, convert it to [CSV](/documentation/features/csv) first — most spreadsheet apps can re-export with a comma delimiter.

If first-class TSV support would be useful for you, [open an issue](https://github.com/2anki/2anki.net/issues) and we'll prioritise it.
